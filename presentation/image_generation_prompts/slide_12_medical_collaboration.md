# Slide 12 Medical Collaboration Flow Prompt

Create a 16:9 Chinese process diagram titled "让检测结果进入医患协同流程". The image should show how AI detection results become useful in a doctor-patient collaboration workflow.

Use a horizontal five-step flow:
1. "患者上传胸片"
2. "AI 检测与解释"
3. "医生推荐"
4. "管理员审核确认"
5. "医生接诊与病例管理"

For each step, include one concise supporting phrase:
- 患者上传胸片: "查看检测结果"
- AI 检测与解释: "标注病灶，整理关键信息"
- 医生推荐: "结合病灶、病史和医生画像"
- 管理员审核确认: "建立医患关系"
- 医生接诊与病例管理: "查看历史，编辑病例"

The visual should emphasize information relay: after AI screening, the result is not isolated; it is passed forward to the doctor with structured context. Use simple medical icons or UI-like cards, but avoid cartoon doctors or generic hospital stock imagery. Keep all Chinese text large and readable.
