# Slide 3 Demand Fit Diagram Prompt

Create a clean 16:9 Chinese presentation diagram titled "需求如何落到系统功能". The diagram should show how three stakeholder groups map to system modules and then to system value. Use Chinese labels in the image.

Structure: three-column flow from left to right.

Left column: "患者", "医生", "管理员". Each role has 2-3 short needs:
- 患者: "上传胸片", "看懂结果", "获得医生推荐"
- 医生: "查看结构化结果", "结合病史判断", "减少报告整理"
- 管理员: "权限管理", "医患关系审核", "模型版本管理"

Middle column: three system modules:
- "模型对话模块": 自然语言问答、报告生成、医生推荐
- "推理管理模块": 单图 / 批量 / ZIP 检测、标注图、风险等级
- "训练与模型管理": 数据集上传、远程训练、模型登记、默认模型切换

Right column: three values:
- "患者侧价值": 更快理解病情
- "医生侧价值": 减少信息整理成本
- "系统侧价值": 权限清晰、模型可迭代

Use arrows or curved connectors to show fit relationships. Prioritize readable relationship mapping over decoration. Keep text concise and large enough for PowerPoint viewing. Avoid repeating long explanations. Use a light medical-tech palette with blue, teal, and warm orange accents.
