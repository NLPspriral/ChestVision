# Slide 1 Cover Image Prompt

Create a clean 16:9 cover slide image for a Chinese student project presentation. The project is named "ChestVision 胸部 X 光片智能辅助分析系统". The visual should immediately communicate "chest X-ray + AI assistance" without overloading the audience.

Layout: left side contains the large title "ChestVision" and the subtitle "胸部 X 光片智能辅助分析系统". Under it, place three compact Chinese keywords: "胸片检测", "智能对话", "医患协同". Right side contains a realistic grayscale chest X-ray panel with a few restrained colored bounding boxes and labels to imply AI detection. The X-ray should look clinical and readable, not decorative or blurred.

Style: academic medical technology, light background, blue-green accents, restrained contrast, generous whitespace, clear hierarchy. Avoid stock-photo doctors, decorative gradients, cartoon characters, excessive icons, and dense paragraphs. The page should feel suitable for a university project defense.

Information carried by image: the viewer should understand that the system turns chest X-ray images into AI-highlighted findings and supports communication.
