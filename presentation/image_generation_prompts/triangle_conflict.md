# Triangle Conflict Diagram Prompt

## Slide 2: Triangle Conflict Diagram

Create a clean 16:9 presentation diagram for a student project about an intelligent chest X-ray assistant system. The visual should show a triangular tension among three healthcare service qualities. Use a light medical-tech background, restrained colors, and a clear academic presentation style. Place the three triangle vertices with the Chinese labels: "专业性" at the top, "随时响应" at the bottom left, and "易理解性" at the bottom right. Add short supporting Chinese captions near each vertex: "医学判断可信" for professionalism, "24h 待命" for always-on responsiveness, and "患者能看懂" for ease of understanding.

Represent the three existing solution types as side labels on the triangle, because each solution covers two qualities and misses the third. On the side between "专业性" and "易理解性", place "医生诊断" with the text "具备：专业性 + 易理解性" and "缺少：随时响应". On the side between "随时响应" and "易理解性", place "大模型问答" with the text "具备：随时响应 + 易理解性" and "缺少：专业性". On the side between "专业性" and "随时响应", place "医学分析平台" with the text "具备：专业性 + 随时响应" and "缺少：易理解性". In the center of the triangle, place the project goal: "ChestVision 目标", with two concise lines: "胸片先结构化，结果再解释" and "必要时把信息接力给医生". Avoid decorative gradients, cartoon characters, stock-photo imagery, and overcrowded text. Make the layout suitable for direct insertion into a PowerPoint slide.
